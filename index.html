FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

1. What are your restaurant's hours of operation?

Answer: Our restaurant is open Monday to Friday from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM, and on weekends from 9:00 AM to 11:00 PM. We also serve brunch on Saturdays and Sundays from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. Please check our website for any special holiday hours.

2. Do you offer vegetarian, vegan, or gluten-free options?

Answer: Yes, we offer a variety of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free dishes. You can find them clearly labeled on our menu, both in-house and online. If you have specific dietary concerns or allergies, please let our staff know, and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.

3. Can I make a reservation online?

Answer: Absolutely! You can easily make a reservation through our website by visiting the "Reservations" section. Alternatively, you can call us directly to book a table. We recommend booking in advance, especially on weekends and holidays, to ensure availability.
